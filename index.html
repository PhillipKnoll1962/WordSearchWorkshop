About & Privacy Policy

Welcome to The Word Search Workshop! This platform is a streamlined utility designed for self-publishers, independent authors, and educators to generate print-ready bulk puzzle books for Amazon KDP or classroom use.

Strict Privacy Standard: No personal information, puzzle text, or uploaded data blocks are ever sent to a server, tracked, or saved.

1. Local Browser Processing

All word lists, titles, grid configurations, and data blocks you process here are handled strictly inside your own web browser window. No data is uploaded to our hosting environment, and nothing is retained once you close or refresh the page.

2. Cookies and Third-Party Advertising

We use Google AdSense to serve advertisements on this website to help support its continued development. Google AdSense uses cookies to serve ads based on a user's prior visits to this website or other websites on the internet. You can opt out of personalized advertising at any time by visiting Google Ad Settings.

3. External Infrastructure

This application is hosted via GitHub Pages. Standard, non-identifying server logs (like page view counters) may be maintained by the hosting provider to ensure technical stability, but no personal information is collected.
