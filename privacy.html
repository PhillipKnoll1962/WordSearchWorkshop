Privacy Policy & About

Last Updated: June 2026

Welcome to The Word Search Workshop. Your privacy is paramount to us. This page outlines how data is handled on our platform and discloses our advertising practices.

1. Data Collection & Processing

The Word Search Workshop is a fully local browser application. We do not operate databases or cloud storage accounts to log your custom puzzle inputs.

  • Puzzle Lists & Text Blocks: All vocabulary words, layout configuration preferences, titles, and generated letter matrix tracks remain completely inside your personal device session.
  • Browser LocalStorage: The app utilizes standard browser localStorage cache targets exclusively to instantly pipe text formats between your active workspace windows. No word list strings are monitored, processed, or mirrored on an external web server framework.

2. Third-Party Advertising & Cookies

We display automated programmatic advertisements via network provider services like Google AdSense to help sustain software hosting costs. Because of this deployment structure:

  • Third-party vendors, including Google, use cookies to serve ads based on a user's prior visits to this website or other sites on the Internet.
  • Google's use of advertising cookies enables it and its partners to serve ads to users based on their visit to our platform and/or other sites across the web.
  • Users may opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google Ads Settings.

3. Local File Security & Image Rendering

Document exports (including printable book layouts and structural target key collections) are computed client-side using localized canvas rendering loops. No tracking mechanisms are embedded within the compiled PDF distribution arrays.

4. Changes to This Privacy Document

We reserve the right to modify this structural disclosure notice at any time to align with dynamic layout adjustments or ad vendor policy modifications. Feel free to monitor structural revisions directly via our platform dashboard access link options.

About the Developer

The Word Search Workshop is custom engineered to streamline indie self-publishing workflows—providing low-content book designers and paperback puzzle creators with an optimized platform for generating high-resolution printable media structures rapidly.
