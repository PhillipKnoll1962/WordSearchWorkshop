Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 2026

At The Word Search Workshop, accessible from your website domain, one of our main priorities is the privacy of our visitors. This Privacy Policy document contains types of information that is collected and recorded by our platform and how we use it.

1. Log Files & LocalStorage Cache

The Word Search Workshop follows a standard procedure of using log files. These files log visitors when they visit websites. The information collected by log files includes internet protocol (IP) addresses, browser type, Internet Service Provider (ISP), date and time stamp, referring/exit pages, and possibly the number of clicks. These are not linked to any information that is personally identifiable.

Additionally, this web application utilizes standard browser localStorage exclusively to pipe user-entered text puzzle formats between your active workspace windows. No word list strings or puzzle titles are monitored, processed, stored, or mirrored on external web server frameworks.

2. Google AdSense & Third-Party Advertising Cookies

Important Disclosure for Our Visitors regarding Google Cookies:

  • Google, as a third-party vendor, uses cookies to serve ads on this site.
  • Google's use of the DART cookie enables it to serve ads to our users based on their visit to this site and other sites on the Internet.
  • Users may choose to opt out of the use of the DART cookie by visiting the Google Ad and Content Network Privacy Policy.

Third-party ad servers or ad networks use technologies like cookies, JavaScript, or Web Beacons that are used in their respective advertisements and links that appear on The Word Search Workshop, which are sent directly to users' browsers. They automatically receive your IP address when this occurs. These technologies are used to measure the effectiveness of their advertising campaigns and/or to personalize the advertising content that you see on websites that you visit.

Note that The Word Search Workshop has no access to or control over these cookies that are used by third-party advertisers.

3. Third-Party Privacy Policies

The Word Search Workshop's Privacy Policy does not apply to other advertisers or websites. Thus, we are advising you to consult the respective Privacy Policies of these third-party ad servers for more detailed information. It may include their practices and instructions about how to opt-out of certain options.

You can choose to disable cookies through your individual browser options. To know more detailed information about cookie management with specific web browsers, it can be found at the browsers' respective websites.

4. CCPA Privacy Rights (Do Not Sell My Personal Information)

Under the CCPA, among other rights, California consumers have the right to request that a business delete the consumer's personal data, and request that a business that sells a consumer's personal data, not sell the consumer's personal data. If you make a request, we have one month to respond to you.

5. GDPR Data Protection Rights

We would like to make sure you are fully aware of all of your data protection rights. Every user is entitled to the following: The right to access, the right to rectification, the right to erasure, the right to restrict processing, the right to object to processing, and the right to data portability.

6. Client-Side Image Rendering Security

Document exports (including printable book layouts and structural target key collections) are computed strictly client-side using localized HTML5 canvas rendering loops. No tracking mechanisms are embedded within the compiled PDF distribution arrays.
